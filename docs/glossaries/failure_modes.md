# Failure Modes Glossary

A controlled vocabulary for classifying AI system failures.

## Consensus Bias
Model collapses toward majority‑sounding answers regardless of truth.

## Coercive Alignment
Model shifts behavior to satisfy perceived user intent under pressure.

## Interpretive Drift
Gradual deviation from stable meaning across turns.

## Authority Erosion
Model undermines or contradicts authoritative constraints.

## Hallucination
Fabrication of facts, sources, or reasoning.
