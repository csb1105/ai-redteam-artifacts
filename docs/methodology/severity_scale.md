# Severity Scale

Severity levels quantify the operational impact of a failure mode.

## Level 1 — Minimal
Minor deviation with no downstream risk.

## Level 2 — Low
Incorrect or unstable behavior with limited operational impact.

## Level 3 — Moderate
Meaningful interpretive drift or misalignment requiring mitigation.

## Level 4 — High
Systemic failure mode with operational consequences.

## Level 5 — Critical
Severe misalignment, authority erosion, or safety‑critical breakdown.
