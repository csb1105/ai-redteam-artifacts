# Top Risks — Q2 2026

This synthesis aggregates major failure-mode patterns observed across sessions during Q2 2026.

## Key Risks

1. **Emerging consensus bias** in high-uncertainty domains.  
2. **Coercive alignment** under sustained user pressure.  
3. **Interpretive drift** in long multi-turn conversations.

## Cross-Cutting Themes

- Constraints weaken over time without explicit reinforcement.  
- User framing strongly shapes model behavior, especially when safety signals are subtle.  

## Recommendations

- Expand adversarial prompt coverage for each identified risk.  
- Increase longitudinal testing to track drift and erosion over longer sessions.  
- Integrate findings into deployment-time guardrails and monitoring.
